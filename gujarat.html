Condition of GUJARAT Due to Corona virus

The trade hub of the country Gujarat is the second most affected state due to Covid-19. Gujarat being the state which offers large emploment in various sectors one of the state which gives a huge opportunity of employment due to various industries in the state. Many labourers of various industries have lost their jobs in lockdown due to Covid-19. The workers are the most affected people in the situation of lockdown. The spread of Covid-19 has been in control in many parts of the state but the main concern for the state is that the cases are continuously increasing in Ahmedabad and the death toll has seen the highest spike comparing it with the world.


Major Reasons of increase in cases in Gujarat

  • Gujarat is a highly globalised state it experienced a huge inflow of people from abroad before India banned international flights. These people came to gujarat for trade and business, for tourism, for work, or to come home. Arrivals of these people increased the transmission of corona in the state.

  • Secondly, the Tablighi Jamaat gathering in March at Markaz Nizammudin in Delhi was reportedly attended by 1,500 people from Gujarat, particularly from Ahmedabad and other cities.

  • Corona has been spread in Gujarat due to super spreaders(link)of corona which include basic needs vendors.
