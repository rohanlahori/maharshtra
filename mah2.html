    Economical effects On Maharashtra Due to Covid-19

    • The revenue received by the State through non-GST sources in April in financial year 2018-19 and 2019-20 was ₹7,829.44 crore and ₹8,251.2 crore, respectively. However, this year, the receipts for April are barely ₹3,314.42 crore.

    • Agriculture being one of the main source of economy for the state it has faced huge losses due to Covid-19

    • The growth of Maharashtra's economy is expected to grow at 5.7% in 2019-20,lowest in past eight years

    • The growth rate of industrial and service sector is expected to show a declination this year.
